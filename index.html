TOMI THE CAT

ABOUT

Hi, my name’s Tomi! (pronounced “toe-mee”)

I’m a male Bengal cat and I was born on January 25th, 2023.

I have 2 sisters, one looks almost identical to me and the other does not. On May 19th, Erin Liu and her family were deciding who to bring home out of us 3, and I got chosen :)

MOMENTS

This was me when I first arrived at Erin and her family’s house! I was almost 4 months old. It was scary being in a new space with new faces, but I got over it eventually.
I loved playing hide-and-seek behind curtains! It was so easy for me to hide since I’m so small.
Nap time is one of my favourite times. I liked sleeping on people’s laps. But I outgrew that phase now that I’m getting bigger.
Looking out windows is one of my biggest hobbies. I like watching the birds and cars go by. I also make sure no bad guys come to my neighbourhood.
When I'm not busy looking out windows, I'm probably staring at the fish that my family owns and wishing I could eat them.
When the weather is nice, I get to go play in our backyard! I love smelling the plants, rolling around and sunbathing on the ground.
My family loves dressing me up. For Halloween I was a pumpkin and for Christmas I’ll be Santa Claus!
I also have a few hats, one of them is a pink flower! I don’t really like wearing outfits but I behave anyways because I know I’ll get treats after.
